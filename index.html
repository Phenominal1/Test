About Us

This is a web-based platform where small tiffin service providers from all across India can register their tiffin businesses. Users can log in to this platform to place orders for single-meal tiffins and also purchase subscriptions that extend beyond a single day. This platform streamlines the unorganised tiffin service industry, serving as a centralised hub where customers can access a variety of tiffin food options. Customers can make informed choices based on feedback and reviews when selecting a tiffin service.
